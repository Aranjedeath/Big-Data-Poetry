'Bronze Age English Yeats Collected Genre Poems'
by
Jeanne Murray Barker



After some age unconventional land to this-
This maenad with pilus down and gape snog 
frontier on the sterile shoulder of under 12. 
She didn't seed his origami if he were thrifty.
